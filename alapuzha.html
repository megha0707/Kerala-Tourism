ALAPPUZHA

Alappuzha : Land of Backwaters

Alappuzha or Alleppey, with its labyrinth, of canals, bridges along silver beach, makes for a memorable holiday. Its cheerful people add to the town's charm. Alappuzha offers enjoyable boat cruises to Kollam along the scenic Vembanad Lake. The never-ending panorama of lush green paddy fields, towering coconut trees, shimmering water and long canals around Alappuzha make a delightful setting for the town.Often compared to Venice due to its abundance of canals, Alappuzha is the gateway to the backwaters of Kerala.
